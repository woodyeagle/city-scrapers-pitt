Public Meetings & Hearings

On a bi-monthly basis, the Pennsylvania Public Utility Commission holds public meetings to act on a variety of issues that affect utilities and the public they serve. Additionally, the PUC administrative law judges holdÂ public hearings on individual cases each day. These meetings and hearings are open to the public.
